Our History

What started as a home-brew kit, has evolved into the bar you see today. At Char's Bar, we strive to be different. From our own brewed beer, to our hand-crafted burgers, you're bound to find something new to enjoy.

Our Featured Famous Burgers and Sandwhiches

All burgers and sandwhiches served with your choice of fries, truffle fries (+$2), or sweet potato fries(+$1).
